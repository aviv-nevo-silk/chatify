Chatify

An Outlook add-in that re-renders an email thread as a WhatsApp-style group chat. Forwarded chains expand into separate bubbles, signatures and meeting boilerplate are stripped, and threads with mixed Hebrew / English render with per-bubble RTL.

Install in Outlook

Sideload the manifest at ./manifest.xml. In Outlook on the Web: My add-ins → Add a custom add-in → Add from File, then upload the downloaded manifest.xml. (The "Add from URL" option is disabled on free outlook.com accounts.)

For org-wide rollout, an M365 admin can upload the manifest via Microsoft 365 admin center → Integrated apps → Upload custom apps. Permission required: ReadItem.

What works

  • Forwarded-chain expansion — one email with a deep forward becomes N bubbles
  • Group header with avatar, subject, participants
  • Signature, calendar-invite boilerplate, and disclaimer stripping
  • "X added Y to the conversation" inline events at the right moment
  • Per-bubble RTL detection for Hebrew / English mixed threads
  • Inline image rendering (https-hosted attachments)
  • Full-screen viewer mirrors the task pane in a wide browser tab
